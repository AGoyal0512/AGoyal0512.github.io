Research

My research interests range across various domains under machine learning and deep learning, including development and utilization of Matrix and Tensor Decomposition techniques to understand emergent or latent properties of complex systems, Network Compression (model sparsity), Computer Vision (medical imaging and model reprogramming), and Natural Language Processing (cognitive sciences, simulating generative agent worlds). I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Hanbaek Lyu and Dr. Junjie Hu.

My other collaborators include Siddharth Suresh, Yun-Shiuan Chuang (UW-Madison), Diganta Misra (CMU), Richard Paul Yim (UCSF), and Pin-Yu Chen (IBM-Research).
